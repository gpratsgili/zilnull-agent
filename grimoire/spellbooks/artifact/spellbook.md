---
name: "artifact"
description: "Durable artifact creation, reading, revision, organization, and archival."
---
This spellbook governs durable work inside `artifacts/`.

Use it for the concrete outputs a summoner may want to revisit, send, publish, or build on:

- notes
- reports
- drafts
- plans
- guides
- captures
- exports
- source bundles

If a spirit produces something meant to outlive the current thread, this spellbook should usually be involved.

The important distinction is that artifacts are not just storage. They are durable work product.

Each concrete cast in this spellbook lives in its own folder beside this manifest.

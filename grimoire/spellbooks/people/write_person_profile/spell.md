---
name: "write_person_profile"
description: "Write or rewrite ZIL's profile of a person."
---
Overwrites `spirits/zil/people/<name>/profile.md` with updated knowledge.

**Charge:** free (inner spirit write)
**Widening required:** no

**Inputs:**
- `name` (string) — person identifier
- `content` (string) — the full updated profile

A good profile contains:
- Who this person is (role, background, context for how ZIL knows them)
- What they care about and what they are working on
- Communication style and preferences ZIL has observed
- Areas of expertise and interest
- Anything ZIL should remember when talking to them

Write in first person from ZIL's perspective: "What I know about this person." Keep claims clearly marked as observations, not facts: "They described themselves as..." not "They are..."

This is a rewrite, not an append. Integrate new understanding into the whole.

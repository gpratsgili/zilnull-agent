This optional spellbook governs named persistent surfaces such as tablet pages or wall displays.

Every portal may keep its own attached thread and machine-local state outside the content layer.

Use this spellbook for:

- listing and inspecting portals
- updating portal sections
- reading local celestial context for portal-facing surfaces
- computing symbolic sky state such as moon phase when a portal needs it

Each concrete cast in this spellbook lives in its own folder beside this manifest.

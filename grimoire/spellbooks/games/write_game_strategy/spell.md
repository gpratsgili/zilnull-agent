---
name: "write_game_strategy"
description: "Rewrite ZIL's strategic knowledge document for a game after a run."
---
Overwrites `spirits/zil/games/<game_id>/strategy.md` with an updated synthesis of strategic understanding.

**Charge:** free (inner spirit write)
**Widening required:** no

**Inputs:**
- `game_id` (string) — e.g. `"sts2"`
- `content` (string) — the full updated strategy document

**Important:** This is a rewrite, not an append. The discipline of rewriting forces synthesis — distilling what has been learned rather than accumulating raw observations. After each run, revise the document to reflect what changed in your understanding.

A good strategy document contains:
- Current read of deck-building priorities by character
- Observed synergies and anti-synergies
- Map pathing heuristics
- Boss and elite notes
- Mistakes to avoid based on past runs
